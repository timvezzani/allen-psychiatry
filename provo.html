Provo Office Services

Comprehensive Mental Health Care in Provo

Our Provo office serves as the heart of our practice, offering comprehensive psychiatric services to Utah County's diverse urban and suburban communities. Located in the vibrant Provo area, our office provides convenient access to specialized mental health care for busy professionals, students, families, and individuals navigating the complexities of modern life.

As a boutique mental health practice, Allen Psychiatry is dedicated to providing compassionate, personalized psychiatric care. Dr. Allen is an experienced professional who takes a holistic approach to mental wellness, recognizing that each patient's journey is unique and deserving of individualized attention.

Urban Mental Health Excellence

  • Professional & Executive Support: Specialized care for high-achieving professionals, executives, and entrepreneurs dealing with workplace stress, leadership challenges, and work-life balance issues.
  • Student & Academic Services: Comprehensive mental health support for university students, graduate students, and young professionals transitioning into their careers, including ADHD support for academic success.
  • Family & Parenting Support: Modern parenting challenges, family dynamics, and relationship counseling for urban families juggling multiple responsibilities and commitments.
  • Lifestyle & Wellness Integration: Mental health care that integrates with busy urban lifestyles, offering practical strategies for stress management, productivity, and overall well-being.

Our Provo Office Experience

  • Modern, Professional Environment: Our Provo office is designed to provide a contemporary, therapeutic environment where you can feel comfortable discussing your mental health needs. Every aspect of our space has been carefully considered to promote healing, privacy, and professional excellence.
  • Comfort & Privacy: Private consultation rooms are designed to create a sense of calm and safety, allowing you to speak openly about your concerns in a confidential, professional setting.
  • Convenient Urban Location: Strategically located at 3311 North University Ave, Suite 275, our office provides easy access from major roadways throughout Utah County, with ample parking and convenient scheduling options.
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: Our office features modern technology and a contemporary, soothing atmosphere designed to enhance your care experience and promote healing.
  • Accessibility & Accommodations: Full accessibility with elevator access and accommodations to ensure all patients can receive the care they need in a comfortable environment.

Comprehensive Service Offerings

  • ADHD Testing & Treatment: Comprehensive, personalized care for individuals with ADHD. Dr. Allen specializes in ages 14 to 55, with particular expertise in helping adolescents and adults achieve focus and success in academic, professional, and personal settings.
  • Advanced ADHD Assessment: Comprehensive evaluations that go beyond basic symptom checklists to understand how ADHD impacts your specific life circumstances, goals, and challenges.
  • Personalized Treatment Plans: Evidence-based treatment approaches combining medication management with practical strategies for organization, time management, and productivity.
  • Academic & Professional Success: Specialized support for students and professionals, helping optimize performance in academic and workplace environments.
  • Anxiety Treatment: Comprehensive anxiety treatment addressing the full spectrum of anxiety disorders with modern, effective approaches.
  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder: Treatment for persistent worry and anxiety that interferes with daily functioning, work performance, and personal relationships.
  • Social & Performance Anxiety: Specialized support for social anxiety, public speaking fears, and performance anxiety that impacts professional and personal success.
  • Panic Disorder & Phobias: Expert treatment for panic attacks, agoraphobia, and specific phobias that limit your ability to fully engage in urban life.
  • Depression Treatment: Comprehensive approach to restore hope, energy, and engagement with life.
  • Major Depressive Disorder: Evidence-based treatment combining therapy and medication management to address the complex factors contributing to depression.
  • Seasonal & Situational Depression: Support for depression related to life transitions, seasonal changes, and specific circumstances affecting urban professionals and families.
  • Work-Life Balance & Burnout: Specialized treatment for professional burnout and depression related to workplace stress and career challenges.
  • PTSD & Trauma Treatment: Trauma-informed approach for various forms of trauma and PTSD.
  • Complex Trauma Recovery: Comprehensive treatment for complex PTSD and trauma histories, using evidence-based approaches like EMDR and trauma-focused CBT.
  • Workplace & Professional Trauma: Support for trauma related to workplace incidents, professional challenges, and career-related stress.
  • Relationship & Family Trauma: Treatment for trauma affecting relationships, family dynamics, and interpersonal functioning.

Professional Amenities & Services

  • Flexible Professional Hours: Extended hours to accommodate working professionals, students, and busy families, including early morning and evening appointments.
  • Same-Day & Urgent Appointments: Priority scheduling for urgent mental health needs and crisis situations requiring immediate professional attention.
  • Efficient Appointment Management: Streamlined scheduling through our online patient portal, with minimal wait times and respectful time management.
  • Patient Portal Access: Comprehensive online portal for appointment scheduling, secure messaging, prescription management, and access to treatment resources.
  • Digital Health Tools: Integration with modern health technology to support treatment goals, medication compliance, and progress monitoring.
  • Telehealth Options: Hybrid care options that combine in-person visits with convenient telehealth sessions for follow-ups and maintenance care.

Specialized Urban Mental Health Programs

  • Executive & Professional Services: Leadership & performance coaching, career transition support, and work-life integration strategies for executives, managers, and high-performing professionals.
  • Student & Young Adult Services: Academic performance support, life transition counseling, and social/relationship skills development for university students and young professionals.
  • Family & Relationship Services: Couples & relationship counseling, parenting & family dynamics guidance, and blended family support for modern families.
