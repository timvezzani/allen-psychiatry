TeleHealth Services

Professional mental health care from the comfort and privacy of your own space, designed for the unique needs of Utah's rural and urban communities.

Mental Health Care That Fits Your Life

At Allen Psychiatry, we understand that getting to an in-person appointment isn't always possible or convenient. Whether you're managing a busy farming schedule, caring for family, or simply prefer the privacy of your own home, our TeleHealth services bring expert psychiatric care directly to you.

Designed for Rural Utah

We recognize the unique challenges faced by rural communities - from geographic isolation to the demands of agricultural life. Our TeleHealth platform bridges these barriers, providing the same high-quality mental health care you would receive in our Provo office, delivered through secure video technology that respects your schedule and privacy.

  • Flexible Scheduling Around Your Life: Early morning sessions before chores, evening appointments after the day's work, and flexible timing that respects seasonal agricultural demands and family responsibilities.
  • Complete Privacy & Discretion: No waiting rooms, no parking lot encounters - receive care from the privacy of your home, ranch, or office. Perfect for close-knit communities where privacy matters.
  • Crisis Support When You Need It: During challenging times like drought, market downturns, or family crises, immediate support is available through our secure platform without the stress of travel.
  • Technology That Works for You: Simple, user-friendly platform that works on smartphones, tablets, or computers - no technical expertise required. We'll guide you through the setup process.

Our TeleHealth Services Include

  • Initial psychiatric evaluations
  • ADHD assessments
  • Medication management appointments
  • Follow-up consultations
  • Crisis interventions
  • Therapeutic support sessions

Platform Features

  • Secure & HIPAA Compliant: Our telehealth platform uses advanced encryption and security measures to protect your privacy and maintain complete confidentiality throughout your session.
  • Accessible from Anywhere: Whether you're at home, on the ranch, or traveling, connect from any location with reliable internet. We serve communities across Utah County, Sevier County, and beyond.
  • Flexible & Convenient: Schedule appointments that work around your life - early mornings, evenings, weekends, and emergency sessions available to accommodate your unique needs.

Getting Started with TeleHealth

  1. Step 1: Schedule Your Appointment
    Use our online scheduling system or call our office to book your TeleHealth session at a time that works for your schedule.
  2. Step 2: Receive Your Secure Link
    We'll send you a secure, private link via email along with simple instructions for joining your appointment.
  3. Step 3: Connect and Begin Care
    Click your link at the scheduled time using any device with a camera and microphone. We'll handle the rest.
  4. Step 4: Continue Your Journey
    Follow-up appointments, medication management, and ongoing support - all available through our convenient TeleHealth platform.

Who Benefits from Our TeleHealth Services?

  • Rural Community Members: Farmers, ranchers, and rural families who face long travel distances to access mental health care can now receive expert treatment without leaving their community.
  • Busy Professionals & Parents: Those with demanding schedules who struggle to fit traditional appointments into their day can access care during convenient times.
  • Individuals Seeking Privacy: People who prefer the discretion of receiving care at home, especially in smaller communities where privacy is important.
  • Those Needing Consistent Care: Patients who require regular follow-ups for medication management or ongoing therapeutic support without travel barriers.
